Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Valorant Points, accounts, delivery, and checkout.

What is Riot Shop?

Riot Shop is a Valorant shop where you can buy Valorant Points and full access Valorant accounts across multiple regions. We have completed 5,000+ orders with fast delivery, clear pricing, and responsive support.

How fast is delivery?

Delivery is instant after payment confirmation. Valorant Points arrive as a gift card code by email, and accounts ship with full login details as soon as your payment clears.

How are Valorant Points delivered?

Valorant Points are delivered through a gift card code sent to your email after checkout. Redeem the code in-game to add points to your account.

Can I preview an account before buying?

Yes. Every listing has a Preview button that opens the full account page with skins, rank, and level details. All accounts are full access with the original email provided.

Is Riot Shop affiliated with Riot Games?

No. We are an independent Valorant shop. VALORANT and Riot Games are trademarks of Riot Games, Inc.

What if something goes wrong with my order?

Contact us through the Reach Us page. If anything goes wrong with your order, we will make it right or issue a full refund. Accounts also include a lifetime warranty.

Are accounts full access?

Yes. Every account includes full access, the original email, and a lifetime warranty. You can change the password and secure the account yourself.

Is there any ban risk?

No. Buying Valorant Points or accounts through Riot Shop does not put you at ban risk.

Do you offer custom Valorant Points amounts?

Yes. Send us a message through Reach Us with the amount you need and we will provide a one-time checkout link.
